# 💰 OX-1 Commercial & Pricing Strategy

## 1. Value-Based Pricing Model
The OX-1 is not a commodity hardware; it is a strategic infrastructure component.
- **MSRP (Suggested Retail Price):** $19,500 USD.
- **Target Gross Margin:** > 90% at scale.

## 2. Revenue Streams
### **A. Hardware Sales (CAPEX)**
Direct sale of the physical OX-1 PCIe card to research institutes and data centers.
### **B. Firmware Licensing (OPEX)**
Annual subscription for the $N_{s,q}$ dynamic calibration updates and $a_2$ spectral libraries.
### **C. Per-Tunneling Royalty**
Micro-payments ($0.05/cycle) for commercial quantum computation runs.

## 3. Production Scaling
- **Phase 1 (Prototyping):** High cost, funded by KOSGEB/Grants.
- **Phase 2 (Small Batch):** 100 units, targeted at Tier-1 University Labs.
- **Phase 3 (Mass Adoption):** Foundry partnership (TSMC/PCBWay) for global distribution.

## 4. Auditor's Financial Risk Assessment
- **Risk:** High R&D cost before first sale.
- **Mitigation:** Focus on the "UST-Core SDK" (Software) first, as it has zero marginal cost of distribution. Use software revenue to fund hardware iterations.
