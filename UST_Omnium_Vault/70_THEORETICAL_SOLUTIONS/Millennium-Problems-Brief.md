# UST Perspectives on Millennium Problems

### 1. Collatz Conjecture (3n+1)
- **UST View:** A "Graceful Shutdown" algorithm. Every string must collapse to the ground state ($1$) to satisfy the $S=0$ equilibrium.

### 2. Navier-Stokes (Turbulence)
- **UST View:** Turbulence is "Baryonic Decoherence." It occurs when local velocity exceeds the $N_{s,q}$ stabilization threshold, causing a buffer overflow in the manifold's fluid dynamics.

### 3. Yang-Mills (Mass Gap)
- **UST View:** Mass is "Processing Latency." The gap represents the energy required for a packet to transit from the Omnium Source into the observable Channel Q.
